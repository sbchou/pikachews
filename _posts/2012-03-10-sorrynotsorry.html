Man, it feels like forever since I've posted!

It's been, as always, crazy busy around these parts but today marks the advent of Spring Break (hallelujah!), a whole week to sleep and cook and eat (you can tell i've got my priorities straight), so there'll be new baking adventures soon, I promise!

Instead of attempting to come up with a coherent apology and description of what's been taking up my time, I figure I'd just say it the way I know best: in pictures!

And the most exciting and recent preoccupation: The Daily Beast's Women in the World Conference 2012!

I had the incredible opportunity as a Columbia student for a free theatre pass-- the event has been truly amazing and inspiring  so far (Hilary Clinton and Meryl Streep are speaking tomorrow!)

Speakers have included Sheryl Sandberg of Facebook, Jill Abramson (editor of the New York Times), Angelina Jolie, Nobel Laureate Leymah Gbowee, feminist Gloria Steinem, Nancy Pelosi, Madeleine Albright, and so many others...

All set in the classy David H Koch theatre!

It's been truly an honor to attend, and reminded me to be so incredibly grateful for what I have...but definitely also sadly aware of how often there still exists a glass ceiling (as Madeleine said, often a "thick wall of men") for women, especially in business and politics.
