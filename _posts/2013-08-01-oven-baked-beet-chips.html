I'm a huge fan of all sorts of roasted veggies-- but these have to be my favorite. The key is to slice them paper thin, and use a lower (350F) temperature, for amazing beautiful crispiness and a sweet taste. These make a lovely presentation-- the color is fantastic!

Roasted Beet Chips
Serves 2

* 2 large beets
* olive oil
* salt, pepper

1. Preheat oven to 350F. Note: I found this lower-than usual temperature for roasting veggies to be the best for optimizing crispiness without burning (at 400F, the chips burned too quickly).
2. Scrub beets, peel, and slice *very* thinly, a mandoline is best.
3. Dump beets onto baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil and salt and pepper. Toss thoroughly, then lay out in *one thin layer, no overlap*.
4. Bake for ~15 min, flip, and bake for ~15 minutes more, until lighter in color, considerably smaller in size, and crispy.
