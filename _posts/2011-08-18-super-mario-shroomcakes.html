If you haven't guessed already, I'm a bit of a Super Mario geek. I just love the sheer creativity of the games. Nothing says childhood like playing the Original Super Mario Bros on my cousin's hand-me-down dog-chewed NES.

Here's a quick and clever little recipe for Mini Mario 'Shroomcakes!
It's really more of a concept. So easy and adorable.
It'd be great for a child's birthday party. Or a not-child's birthday party.Or perhaps to give your friend a Power-up? We could all use one of those.

1-UP AND POWER-UP CAKES

Makes 24 mini-shrooms

• ½ batch of your favorite White or Yellow Cake Batter
• ½ batch of your Favorite Frosting
• white chocolate chips, for decoration (at most, 1 cup)
• red and green food coloring
• black marker

1. This is more of a concept than a recipe! And a very cute one at that.
2. Line a mini-muffin tin with paper cups and fill them about ½ full with batter. Bake for approx. 10 min, or until risen and firm. Move cakes to a wire rack and let them cool completely.
3. Meanwhile, prepare the buttercream according to instructions. Spoon into two bowls; add red food coloring to one, and green food coloring to the other. You want a nice vibrant color.
4. When cupcakes are cool, frost with color of choice, and stick a few white chocolate chips, pointy-side down, into frosting to make the spots. Use the marker to draw two little eyes on the paper liner. Voila!
