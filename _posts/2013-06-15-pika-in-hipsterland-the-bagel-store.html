A sharp contrast to the vibe at Variety Cafe, The Bagel Store looks much more like your average hole-in-the-wall manhattan deli, with an attitude to match. No tattoed hipsters, no apathetic cute baristas, just brusque service, and damn good bagels. Just the way I like it.

WHAT TO GET:

The Bagel Store is famous for their unique flavors, especially the extremely rich Bacon, Egg & Cheese bagel (all baked right in!) and the French Toast bagel. As I have a sweet tooth extraordinaire, I got the French Toast bagel with Blueberry Cheesecake cream cheese, untoasted please. (They also have a wide assortment of cream cheese flavors!). The bagel had a lovely crispy shell, dusted with plentiful powdered sugar, and a cinnamon-raisin inside. The cream cheese was rich and delightful. It's definitely less traditional than my other favorite store, Absolute near Columbia, but the flavors are well executed and the texture is straight-on. Next time, I'll give the plain bagel + cream cheese a try for a more standard comparison.

THE LOW DOWN:

The Bagel Store

754 Metropolitan Ave
(between Graham Ave & Humboldt St)
Brooklyn, NY 11211

Pros: Creative flavors, good execution, well-priced

Cons: Cash only

Recommended: yes!
