Easy Bean & Cheese Quesadillas  

Serves 1

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • ¼ c refried beans
  • ¼ c shredded cheese
  1. On half of the tortilla, spread a thick layer of refried beans. Sprinkle with cheese and fold in half.
  2. Microwave on high for about 30 seconds, or until cheese melts and quesadilla is heated through.
  3. Cut into halves, and enjoy!
