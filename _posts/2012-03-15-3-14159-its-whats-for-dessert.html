Chocolate Peanut Butter Mousse Pie.
Oh my goodness.
So rich.
So good.
And so easy! Did I mention it's no-bake? And vegan!

Here's how:

1. Make your graham cracker crust. Any ol' recipe will do. Mine was ad-lib.

2. Pat it into the pie pan (9", please), and stick it in the fridge. Meanwhile..

3. Whip up your PB mousse in the food processor! I used this one, except halving the PB for a lighter taste. Oh, also make only 1/2 the recipe since you want chocolate too! Scoop out into a bowl.

4. Now make the chocolate part! I used a modification of this recipe, (halved, of course!) although. It's one of my absolute favorite decadent desserts and there are different variations all over the interwebs.

5. Put alternating dollops of both mousses into the prepared crust.

6. Now swirl it up!

7. To take it over the top, drizzle liberally with melted chocolate! Now, cover with plastic wrap and put the whole shebang back in the fridge for at least 2-3 hours, or until ready to serve.

8. ENJOY! Celebrate Pi Day!
