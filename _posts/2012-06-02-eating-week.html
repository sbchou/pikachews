This week has left me, in a word, very, very, full

. And also, very, very excited to spend my Friday night in bed with the Netflix.

This week, there was...

a lot of ice cream consumed. Goddammit, when the mercury rises New York becomes a veritable Sophie-trap with ice cream trucks on every corner. I have never been able to resist a good cone on the way home from work, and since this isÂ Manhattan, we're not talking about the good stuff, not just Mr. Softee (although that always hits the spot!). This particular delightful specimen is from Amornino Gelato, near Union Square. It's Dark Chocolate, Tiramisu, and Hazelnut. Isn't she a beaut?

an overload of cuteness at the office. I swear to God, the company I work at could win some sort of cute dog competition. This here is Squishy the pug, and she lives up to her name indeed.

many a bear at Bare Burger a delicious organic burger mini-chain in NYC. (But no actual bears harmed in the noshing.)

I opted for the veggie option and was NOT disappointed--BareBurger makes a mean meatless patty topped with lots of melty cheese, creamy avocado (my fave!), all on a sturdy multigrain bun.

It's called the California, and it was sunny indeed.

I couldn't resist trying one of their organic sodas on the menu, and the Blueberry Soda was the perfect touch to a delicious meal.

Both wontons and ramen in my soup for dinner. Can't make up your mind? Then go for the Shrimp Wonton Noodle Soup at Noodle Village, in Chinatown. One note: it is a bit on the blander side, (although it's a good sign if you're tired of over-salty, MSG-laden packaged ramen).

And finally, another cute pup to close out the day, this one I spotted on the floor of a hair salon in C-town. She even had little barrettes in her fur!
