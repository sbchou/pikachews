How cute are these Hello Kitty themed cupcakes??

I got the cupcake-making kit at Target, my favorite place to shop for odds-and-ends in Suburbia.

Each paper cupcake cup has a picture of Kitty's face on the bottom + little designs of her adventures on the side.

Even after baking, the colors stay nice and bright!

 I used my favorite vegan white cake recipe.

It comes with Hello Kitty candy sprinkles and paper flags! Squee!

Seriously these cupcakes make me so happy.

I shared them with my suite for a little pep-up on labor day the night before classes began.

In other Hello Kitty food news, check out this happy ad for Beard Papa on the Upper West side! If you're in Manhattan or Columbia and have never been, I urge you all to go ASAP! I never thought much of cream puffs but these are heavenly: perfectly crispy on the outside and creamy on the inside, and light as air. Plus they have a fun featured flavor every week: when I went it was chocolate, and I'll have to go back to try the green tea!
