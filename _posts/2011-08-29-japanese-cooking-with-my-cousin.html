My awesome cousin Alice and I are both sorta obsessed with Japanese culture.

Last winter we were lucky enough to visit Tokyo for a trip-of-a-lifetime before the earthquake and tsunami sadly devastated the beautiful country. The food was absolutely amazing! Everything was made with eye to detail and presentation. Sushi in the US really can't compare! I always feel like rice in American restaurants is never quite made right. I would love to go back one day, but for now, we'll have to make do with our own homemade sushi and okonomiyaki!
