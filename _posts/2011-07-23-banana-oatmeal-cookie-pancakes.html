These pancakes from Chocolate Covered Katie taste like one part oatmeal cookie + one part caramelized banana + one part melted chocolate. Last time I checked, that equation = the perfect way to start your weekend.

Banana Oatmeal Cookie Pancakes (Vegan)
Makes 1 large serving, about 3 pancakes
adapted from Chocolate Covered Katie

  • 1/3 cup white whole wheat flour
  • 1/3 cup oatmeal
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1 T brown sugar
  • 1 t cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 t baking powder
  • 1/2 cup soymilk
  • 1/2 banana, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  1. Lightly grease a skillet and preheat over medium heat.
  2. Meanwhile, stir together all dry ingredients in a bowl, and all the wet in another. Add the wet into the dry and stir just to combine. Stir in chocolate chips.
  3. Ladle about ¼ cups of batter onto preheated skillet and top each pancake with a few slices of banana. Cook for 2-3 min. or until bubbles form along the top and edges are firm. Flip and cook for another 2-3 min.
  4. Serve warm.

Note: the original recipe from Chocolate Covered Katie contains no added oil, but if you're not watching your fat intake, I'd add 1 tsp oil to the batter for more moist pancakes (and hey, there's plenty of chocolate so you might as well give in, right?).
