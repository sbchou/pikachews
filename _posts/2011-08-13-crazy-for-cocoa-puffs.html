Cocoa Banana Pancake Puffs
Makes 7 mini puffs
(perfect for a weekend breakfast or brunch!)

  • 1/4 c + 2 T whole wheat flour
  • 2 T cocoa powder
  • 1 t baking powder
  • 1 T sugar
  • a dash of cinnamon
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1/2 c soymilk
  • 1 t vegetable oil
  • banana slices
  1. Grease ebelskiver pan liberally and preheat over a medium flame.
  2. In a large cereal bowl, mix together flour, cocoa, baking powder, sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Pour in soymilk and oil, and stir just until combined.
  3. Drop a tablespoon of batter into each indent, top with a banana slice, and cover with a bit more batter. Cook for 3-5 min, until edges are set, and flip over with chopsticks/tool of choice. Cook 2-3 min more, and carefully remove. Serve warm. Watch your mood miraculously improve.
