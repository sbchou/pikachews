On Saturday, we went to Smorgasburg, a weekly food festival by the Brooklyn Flea, featuring local sellers!

It was a lovely time, with so many interesting and unique treats to be sampled.

The best part was meeting and interacting with all the different vendors, each who had their own story to tell!
