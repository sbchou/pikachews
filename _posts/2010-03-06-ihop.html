I haven't been to ihop in ages... literally like 10 years. And I'm 17.

The pancakes were tasty (like dessert for breakfast!) and the portions quite large, but at almost $8 for a stack of strawberry-banana pancakes, it was a bit of a rip-off. But great for a delayed opening day.
