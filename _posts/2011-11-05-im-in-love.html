Ridiculously Addictive Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies (Vegan)

Adapted from SparkPeople

Makes 2 dozen medium-large cookies

• 2 c flour
• 1 t baking soda
• 1 t baking powder
• 1 t cinnamon
• ¼ t salt
• 1 c brown sugar
• 1 c pumpkin puree
• ½ c unsweetened applesauce
• 1 c chocolate chips

1. Preheat oven to 350F and line two cookie sheets.
2. In a medium bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
3. In a large bowl, combine sugar, pumpkin, applesauce. Stir in the dry mixture until just combined; fold in chocolate chips.
4. Drop large spoonfulls of batter onto cookie sheet.
5. Bake for about 15 min, or until puffed and firm on top.
