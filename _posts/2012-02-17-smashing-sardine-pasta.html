Enter this speedy pasta dish, made up on the fly with leftovers in the fridge and an oft-overlooked (but incredibly nourishing) pantry staple: sardines. Perfect brain food to get you through even the busiest of days!

Smashing Sardine Pasta
Serves 1 (although it's always handy to double up on pasta for lunch the next day!)

  • 1 can sardines in tomato sauce (about 3-4 oz)
  • 2 oz whole wheat spaghetti
  • 2 c fresh spinach
  • 1 T olive oil
  • 1 T fresh lemon juice (1/2 lemon)
  • generous amount of fresh ground pepper!
  1. In a small pot, bring water to a rolling boil. Cook spaghetti according to box directions until al dente. Drain and coat with olive oil.
  2. In the same pot, steam spinach with a bit of water until soft and wilted. Toss with spaghetti, and drizzle with ample lemon juice and fresh black pepper (be generous here--it really brightens up the dish!).
  3. Top with sardines and sauce.
  4. Enjoy!
