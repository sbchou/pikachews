fash rehash: red floral dress

inspired by Grosgrain’s lovely post, I decided to refashion a little dress for myself out of an old black tanktop and decade-old scraps of pretty red fabric.

Two broken needles, several bouts of seam-ripping, and a day and a half later, I finally finished what was supposed to be a “half-hour” project. The dress is very cute and perfectly summery, though, so it was worth the extra effort. If you’re more experienced at sewing, I’m sure you could whip one up in no time at all!
