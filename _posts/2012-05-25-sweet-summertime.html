Long time no blog, I know, I'm sorry.

It seems like the last load of laundry from school was scarce dry before I was packing up my bags once again and trucking my suitcases back and self back to the City--this time, in the heart of things, right next to Union Square.

This lucky piece of prime real estate was landed for the summer with no small thanks to a great fellowship organization that I am proud to call myself a member of. hackNY, which is now in its 3rd year, seeks to foster a community of young "hackers" and entrepreneurs by providing sponsored housing and a full-time internship at a start-up in New York City to top students/hackers around the nation. Although it is a fun and exciting opportunity, it also leaves little time for baking and blogging!
