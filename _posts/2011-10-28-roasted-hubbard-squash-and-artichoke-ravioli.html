I got the funniest looking squash from the market the other day, isn't it such a bright & cheery color? I couldn't help but buy it.

Turns out it's a hubbard squash. Cut it into hunks and roasted it slathered with olive oil and a smidge of salt and pepper. Not bad! It reminds me a bit of kabocha squash (my favorite!) in texture, but not as sweet. I absolutely adore all sorts of squash.

Served with some artichoke ravioli from Trader Joe's (another thing I absolutely adore), it was a delicious dinner to celebrate the end of the week!
