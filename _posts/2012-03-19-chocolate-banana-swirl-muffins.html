Here's a fun and delicious alternative to a sweet breakfast staple: banana bread with a (chocolate) twist! To make Monday mornings a bit brighter, I also used a cute heart shaped tin to bake the muffins in, although a regular cupcake tin or large loaf pan will work just fine. This recipe is very moist, thanks to the applesauce and plentiful bananas (it contains no added fat or eggs!). The cocoa cake is full of chocolate flavor and makes a delicious and pretty contrast to the bananas.

Chocolate Swirl Banana Bread Muffins (Vegan)

Makes 1 dozen
Adapted from Passion4Eating

  • 4 very ripe bananas
  • 1 snack-pack unsweetened applesauce (1/3 c)
  • ½ c brown sugar
  • 2 t vanilla
  • 1 ½ c AP flour
  • 1 t baking soda
  • a pinch of salt
  • ⅓ c cocoa powder
  1. Preheat oven to 350F and line a muffin tin.
  2. In a large bowl, mash bananas, applesauce, brown sugar, and vanilla.
  3. Sift in flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir until smooth.
  4. Scoop out ½ the batter into another bowl and mix in the cocoa powder.
  5. In each muffin cup, alternate a scoop of banana batter with chocolate batter. Lightly swirl with a knife (don’t overdo it!).
  6. Bake for about 20 min, or until a toothpick comes clean.
