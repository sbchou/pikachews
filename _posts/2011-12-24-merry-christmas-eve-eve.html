Aren't these reindeer cookies just adorable? I used the same peanut butter cookie recipe as for the Peanut Butter Nutella Thumbprints, and it turned out great!

Little Reindeer Cookies
Makes about 15 cookies

  • 1 c AP flour
  • ½ t baking soda
  • ½ t salt
  • ½ c pb
  • ½ c honey
  • ⅙ c oil
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1 bag mini pretzel twists, broken into halves
  • 1 bag m&ms
  • mini chocolate chips
  1. Preheat oven to 350F and line 2 cookie sheets.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, stir together pb, honey, oil, and vanilla until creamy. Stir in dry ingredients until combined.
  4. Roll tbs of dough into balls and place on baking sheets.
  5. Stick two pretzel halves into each dough ball for “antlers”.
  6. Bake for approximately 10 min, and immediately press an m&m into the center of each cookie for the nose, and 2 mini chocolate chips for eyes.
  7. Cool completely before serving!
