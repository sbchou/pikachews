Recently I had the pleasure of joining my lovely roommate and her boyfriend for dinner at Corton NYC in TriBeCa. I'm not going to write a comprehensive review of the tasting menu we enjoyed because, quite frankly, I've never had a dining experience on such an elaborate scale before. Although the cuisine was deemed Nouveau French, there was a definite Eastern influence and it seemed more like Asian fusion. There were 10+ courses in all, and each bite (in order to not overwhelm the stomach, most courses were 1-2 bite creations) was truly a "taste"--really intensified flavor in a small package. Some combinations worked better than others, but all in all, it was an undeniably creative and fun feast.

Big thanks (and a safe trip home!) to Max!
