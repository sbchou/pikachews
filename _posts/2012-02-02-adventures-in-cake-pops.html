Rainbow sprinkles make the world go round

Here's an incredibly belated pictorial post on my adventures making cake pops (well more like little spherical cupcakes, not the usual mashed cake-and-frosting kind) with the lovely Melissa when I visited her home in Miami! Sweet memories of a warmer place :)

The little cake-pop molds

Whipping up cake batter (funfetti, of course!)

Mel the baker

Some of them overexpanded! We ate those pieces off, no biggie :)

Some of them stuck too the pan...glued those back together with chocolate!

Then it was off to the fridge to let the chocolate-dipped sticks set

We dipped them in chocolate ganache made by Mel's talented little sister Ashley, who is quite the chef at 12!

Cake makes me smile!

We made pretty gold-flaked cupcakes with the remaining batter and ganache!
