So i was doing some summer cleaning-- going through the closet and setting out old clothes for donation and whatnot. and i came upon these two long, flowy skirts, not really my style, since i'm so small and all. On a whim i decided to try them on as tube dresses, and with a cinch belt-- voila! perfect summer party dresses. Don't let old skirts go to waste-- who knows, there may be a cute minidress waiting to come out!

P.S.: excuse the crappy lighting and messy room. like i said, it was closet-cleaning time.
