Magic Peanut Sauce

(So this isn't really a recipe, I know...but you really don't need one, I swear! Just eyeball it and give it a taste...trust me :) The amounts are up to you! )

  • creamy peanut butter
  • soy sauce
  • vinegar
  • sugar or honey
  • hot water
  1. In a small bowl, combine peanut butter, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar/honey (all to taste). Slowly add hot water until you reach a pourable consistence.
  2. Use as a sauce, dip, or marinade!

Pictured above is one of my favorite busy weeknight meals: steamed broccoli, tofu, and the aforementioned sauce!
