There is a common stereotype that stereotypes are bad.

Sometimes it feels like I spend a lot of my day and a lot of my life fighting against them.

Plant-lover, but not a tree-hugger.

City girl, small town roots.

Cupcake-making, kitten-obsessed feminist.

Bookworm, writer, engineer. (For the record SEAS can read!*)

Sometimes, it can get rather tiring.

Sometimes, you're not sure whether to be pleased you've fooled them so well or punch the next person in the face who exclaims: "You're an engineer? I thought you were an English major!" Read: because you're a girl and you don't have four-inch glasses. No way!

But just between you and me, stereotypes exist for a reason. Sometimes they're kind of true. Sometimes I even endorse them.

Hello my name is small Asian girl, and Hello Kitty makes me happy. Especially in cookie form. Bonus points for pink-ness and tiny-ness. Kthxbai.
