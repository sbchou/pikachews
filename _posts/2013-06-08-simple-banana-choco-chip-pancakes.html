Simple Chocolate Chip Banana Pancakes
Makes 3 batches of 3 pancakes (9), serves 3

* 2 bananas
* 1.5 c flour
* 1 T baking powder
* pinch of salt
* 1 c milk
* 1 c chocolate chips
* butter

In a large bowl, mash bananas.
Stir in rest of ingredients and add chocolate chips.
Meanwhile, preheat a large skillet and add a pat of butter.
When pan is hot, fry pancakes till browned on each side, flip once.
Repeat.
