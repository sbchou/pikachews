Think that these are just a plate of plain chocolate cookies? Surprise! Think again. Inside every brownie-like cookie is a bite of soft, sweet caramel!

Chocolate Surprise! Cookies
Very loosely adapted from this recipe
Yield: ~2 dozen

Ingredients:
*1/2 c butter
*1 c sugar
*1/2 c cocoa
*2 eggs
*1/4 t salt
*1/2 t baking soda
*2 c AP flour
*24 caramels, unwrapped (Kraft)

Directions:
1. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment and preheat oven to 350F.
2. In a medium pan, melt butter. Take off heat and stir in sugar, cocoa, eggs, and salt. Add baking soda and flour and stir just until combined.
3. Wrap heaped tbsps of dough around caramels and place 2" apart on baking sheet. Make sure to seal the dough all the way around!
4. Bake ~8 minutes, until the top is puffy, but don't overbake so insides remain soft and fudgy!
