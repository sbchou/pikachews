Hello friends & lovers,

It has been an absolutely incredible nearly 4 (!) years eating, cooking, and blogging at Mon Petit Chou. Unfortunately, like all good things, it cannot exist in the same form forever.

Therefore it is with a sad heart that I announce that Mon Petit Chou, the food blog, will be on hiatus for indeterminate amount of time; potentially for good. Because I will be (as always) eating and cooking, I have started a much more streamlined tumblog, Pika Chews, at pika-choux.tumblr.com. The idea is more posts, more pictures, less text. Please follow me!

In the meantime all the archived recipes will continue to exist on the old site and URL.

Here's to good memories and new beginnings,

Sophie
