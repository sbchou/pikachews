Rainy Day BBQ Tofu & Veggie Roast
Makes 3 hearty servings

• 1 block extra firm tofu, cubed
• about ½ cup BBQ sauce
• 2 large potatoes, cut into thick fries
• large bunch of broccoli, floreted
• salt, pepper, olive oil

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (see note). Grease two baking sheets.
2. In separate bowls, toss broccoli & potatoes with oil until well coated. Mix together tofu & BBQ sauce in a third bowl.
3. Put the veggies into one baking sheet, spreading them all out, and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Pour tofu onto another sheet.
5. Bake for 20-30 min, or until tender and browned.
6. Enjoy!

Note:
The oven in my dorm kitchen is a little wonky and tends to underheat so I ended up roasting everything for about 30-40 min, and sadly, the veggies didn't get as crisp and caramelized as they ought to. Next time i'm upping it to 425° and seeing how it goes, but if you are on peaceful terms with your own oven, 400° for 20-30 min. should be just fine and dandy.
