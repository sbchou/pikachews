Schoolwork has definitely been amping up to full volume now and that in combination with attempts to keep a small budget is my excuse for the lack of creative/real recipes lately.

But  sometimes simpler is better!

Here was a quick lunch I had last week: sundried tomato hummus and fresh tomatoes on a slice of baguette. (Tartine is just a fancy French word for open-face sandwich! Sounds nice, doesn't it?)

The best part?

It was almost completely free. I had worked an event at the Deutsches Haus that night and got to take all the yummy leftover hors d'oeuvres home: fresh baguettes, cheese, and this lovely hummus (I adore sundried tomatoes!).

Not much of a recipe, but it was delicious!
