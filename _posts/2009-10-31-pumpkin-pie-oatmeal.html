Pumpkin Pie Oatmeal (makes 1 hearty breakfast)

  • ½ c quick oats
  • 1 c water
  • ½ c pumpkin puree
  • 1-2 T brown sugar
  • 1 t cinnamon or pumpkin pie spice

1. Microwave oats and water for 1-2 minutes, or until cooked.

2. Stir in pumpkin, sugar, and spices (to taste).

3. Eat!
