Without further ado, the recipe:

Banana & Chocolate Chip Stuffed Ebelskivers
Makes 6 pancake-puffs
(perfect for a hearty breakfast or snack!)

  • 1/2 c whole wheat flour
  • 1 t baking powder
  • 1 T sugar
  • a dash of cinnamon
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1/2 c soymilk
  • 1 t vegetable oil
  • a few banana slices and/or some chocolate chips
  1. Grease ebelskiver pan liberally and preheat over a medium flame.
  2. In a large cereal bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Pour in soymilk and oil, and stir just until combined.
  3. Drop a tablespoon of batter into each indent, top with a banana slice (or filling of choice), and cover with a bit more batter. Cook for 3-5 min, until edges are set, and flip over with chopsticks/tool of choice. Cook 2-3 min more, and carefully remove. Serve warm.

Note:

Any old pancake recipe will do, I used my basic quick & easy vegan one. Since my pan is rather petite feel free to double the recipe if you have a standard-sized pan with 7+ holes or would like to be generous and feed more than one person.

If you don't have an ebelskiver pan, do give it a second thought! I usually don't approve of uni-taskers, but there's just so much room for creativity (sweet and savory combinations and fillings).
