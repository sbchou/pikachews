Baozi or Bao aka Stuffed/Steamed Buns are a staple of Chinese cuisine, especially (according to the authoritative source on Chinese food, my mom) Northern cooking, which is wheat-based rather than rice based and has a lovely assortment of all kinds of doughy goods.

But did you ever wonder how those pretty little pleats are formed? Well, wonder no more! Check out this little clip of my mom folding up vegetarian baozi (filled with tofu skin, candied carrots, ginger and scallion) for dinner in record time!
